Guacamole

Ingredients and quantity

Measurement Ingredients
4 avocado
½ cup onion
¼ cup cilantro
1 jalapeño jalapeño
3 spoon lime juice
1 teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon Coriander

Instructions

  1. Using a spoon, scoop the flesh of the avocados into a low serving bowl.
  2. Any bruised, browned areas.
  3. Using a pastry cutter, potato masher, or fork, mash up the avocado until it reaches your desired texture.
  4. Promptly add the onion, cilantro, jalapeño, lime juice, coriander, and salt.
  5. Stir to combine.
  6. Taste and add additional salt, until the flavors really sing.
